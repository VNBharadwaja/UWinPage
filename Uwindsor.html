Your Uwindsor experience will be unique to anyone else's. With so many options and opportunities in front of you, it can be easy to get lost along the way. For that reason, the University of Windsor has created these experiecncedd maps, tailored to your field(s) of study, to help guide you towards your personal objectives. These maps are meant to help you explore the various opportunities throught out the course of your Uwindsor Experience. This is to help you see what you can do, rather than what you are required to do!
